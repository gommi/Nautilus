Building Automation Solutions for HVAC

Custom BMS System and Commercial HVAC Solutions
Designed Just for You

When you want to increase the operational and energy efficiency of your building, a BMS system and the latest commercial HVAC products and technology should be the first solutions to investigate. Nautilus Controls works with you to design, install, integrate, and maintain building management systems that improve the safety, comfort, and security of your commercial properties.

What is a BMS System?

A BMS system is a series of integrated hardware (LINK TO OUR PRODUCTS) and software installed into a building to automate maintenance, energy use, systems monitoring, and other essential functions. It can be customized to exact specifications, including off-site and cloud-based control systems or performing regular testing functions automatically.

What are the Benefits of Having a BMS System?

  • Increase productivity and operational efficiency.
  • Gain easier control of energy use and monitoring of environmental impact.
  • Ensure tenant comfort by automatically switching off equipment when not in use.
  • Can easily share control of the system via smartphones, which allows additional convenience to property managers
  • Get an enterprise-wide view and automation of building control processes.
  • Improve response time to events, which can save money and get things back to normal as soon as possible.
  • Share information quickly and efficiently between departments, keeping everyone on the same page at all times.
  • Meet regulatory compliance, and tenant safety and satisfaction.
  • Qualify for financial incentives for offsetting the cost of significant energy improvements.

    • How Does a BMS System Work with HVAC?

    A BMS system offers you complete control of your building’s HVAC operations. You can monitor heating and cooling functions, energy use, maintenance needs/ schedule, and even address issues in real time.

    Since HVAC use accounts for an estimated 40% of energy use in commercial buildings, having an automated system that helps increase energy efficiency and quickly resolve issues that drain power can help you save on energy costs.

    Nautilus Controls can install a new BMS system that fits your current needs, retrofit your existing system, and provide ongoing maintenance and remote client services once we get you up and running.
